by AksGames is a Game based on basic Mathematics like addition, subtraction, multiplication,division etc….., by playing this you can increase your calculating speed in a fun way, sounds great right? Then why you Waiting for Download Now!

How to Play

There are several levels in this game and all levels except 1 can be played by two people and that too on one device.
At that 2 player level., player blue and player red both are separated by a white line and also there is a striker on the center of the playing area
And both players have a different mathematical question. Those who solve that question quickly, Can win the game. when you answer the questions the striker moves to the opponent’s court , if the player red answers quickly the striker moves to blues court and if it touches to the blue line the red will win in that game. Likewise for player blue. And also there is a timer in this game when the time Hits 0 then it evaluates in which court has the most part of the striker is. if the most part of the striker is in reds court then blue win the game. Likewise for player red.
And you can also play alone. There is a solo level. Which increase your calculating speed and you can score a great Highscore that no one can beat.
